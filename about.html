About Electrical n That

The Business

Run by a licensed electrician, not a call centre

Electrical n That is a Casino-based electrical contracting business run by Scott Matthews, a licensed electrician (Lic. 276769C) covering residential and commercial work across the Northern Rivers.

Scott runs the crew with a first-year and fourth-year apprentice, so most jobs are quoted and delivered by the same people you speak to on the phone — not passed between office staff and subcontractors.

Straightforward, by the numbers

Every quote is based on what the job actually needs. If something's going to cost more than expected, you'll hear about it before it happens, not after.
