Service 03

Switchboard Upgrades

Old ceramic fuse boards don't have safety switches and can't handle modern loads. A switchboard upgrade brings the property up to current safety standard and is often required to sell, lease or insure a property.

  • Replacing ceramic fuses with circuit breakers
  • Safety switch (RCD) installation
  • Board upgrades for solar, EV chargers or air con additions
  • Compliance certificates issued on completion

Service 05

Safety Switches & Smoke Alarm Compliance

NSW landlords are legally required to keep smoke alarms compliant and safety switches installed. We handle the physical work — testing, repairs, replacements — for property managers and owners.

  • Smoke alarm testing, repair and replacement
  • Safety switch installation and testing
  • Landlord compliance certificates
  • Ongoing servicing for real estate agencies

Service 06

Emergency Callouts

Power out, safety switch won't reset, or something's sparking? Call and we'll talk you through what's safe to do until we get there.

  • Safety switch tripping and won't reset
  • Total or partial power loss
  • Burning smell or sparking outlets
  • Storm and water damage electrical faults
